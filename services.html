What We Offer

Our Services

Comprehensive, evidence-based treatment services designed to support every stage of your recovery journey.

01

Detoxification

Medical detoxification is the critical first step in overcoming addiction. At Hugs Life Holistic, detox is conducted in a fully supervised clinical environment to ensure patient safety and comfort throughout the withdrawal process.

  • 24×7 medical monitoring during withdrawal
  • Medication-assisted treatment to ease discomfort
  • Individualised detox protocols based on substance type
  • Vital sign monitoring and emergency response readiness
  • Nutritional support and hydration management
  • Psychological support during the detox phase

Detox alone is not treatment — it is the first step. Our care continues seamlessly into rehabilitation and therapy after detox is complete.

Why Supervised Detox Matters

Attempting to detox alone can be dangerous. Withdrawal from substances like alcohol and benzodiazepines can cause life-threatening seizures. Our medical team ensures the process is safe, managed, and as comfortable as possible.

Safe · Monitored · Compassionate

Dual Diagnosis Care

Many individuals with addiction also suffer from underlying mental health conditions — depression, anxiety, PTSD, or bipolar disorder. Our psychiatrists are trained to identify and treat both conditions simultaneously.

Treating Mind & Addiction Together
02

Psychiatric Support

Mental health is at the core of addiction recovery. Our in-house psychiatrists provide comprehensive evaluation and ongoing psychiatric treatment throughout your stay and into your aftercare phase.

  • Comprehensive psychiatric assessment on admission
  • Diagnosis and treatment of co-occurring mental disorders
  • Prescription and management of psychiatric medications
  • Regular reviews and dosage adjustments
  • Crisis intervention and mental health emergency support
  • Coordination between psychiatric and counselling teams
03

Counselling & Therapy

Therapy is the heart of the rehabilitation process. Our trained counsellors and psychologists use a range of evidence-based approaches to help patients understand their addiction, develop coping skills, and build a foundation for lasting recovery.

  • Individual one-on-one therapy sessions
  • Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)
  • Motivational Enhancement Therapy
  • Group therapy and peer support sessions
  • Family counselling and relationship rebuilding
  • Trauma-informed therapy
  • Life skills and relapse prevention training

Family Counselling

Addiction affects the entire family. We provide dedicated family therapy sessions to improve communication, rebuild trust, and equip loved ones to become a positive force in the recovery journey.

Individual · Group · Family

Mind-Body Healing

Yoga and meditation are scientifically proven to reduce stress, regulate emotions, and improve mental health — all of which are critical for sustained recovery from addiction.

Daily Practice · Expert Guidance
04

Yoga & Meditation

Our holistic healing program incorporates traditional Indian wellness practices — yoga, pranayama, and meditation — to restore balance, build inner resilience, and create healthy daily routines that support sobriety.

  • Daily morning yoga and stretching sessions
  • Pranayama (breathing exercises) for anxiety relief
  • Guided meditation and mindfulness practice
  • Stress management and relaxation techniques
  • Nature walks and therapeutic outdoor activity
  • Spiritual and reflective practices (optional)
05

Residential Stay

Our residential program provides a structured, safe, and nurturing environment away from the triggers and pressures of everyday life. Patients live on-campus, allowing for full immersion in the recovery process with round-the-clock support.

  • Clean, comfortable private and shared rooms
  • Nutritious, balanced daily meals
  • Structured daily schedule with therapy, yoga, and recreation
  • 24×7 nursing and support staff on premises
  • Secure, monitored environment for patient safety
  • Recreational and social activities for wellbeing
  • Regular communication updates for families

A Healing Environment

Our Dehradun centre is set in a peaceful location, away from the chaos of city life. The natural surroundings of Uttarakhand complement the therapeutic process and help patients reconnect with themselves.

Peaceful · Structured · Safe

Relapse Prevention

Research shows that the first 6–12 months after leaving treatment are the most vulnerable for relapse. Our aftercare program provides the ongoing support structure that protects long-term recovery.

Follow-up · Support · Prevention
06

Aftercare Program

Recovery is a lifelong journey. Our aftercare program is designed to support patients after they leave our centre — ensuring they have the tools, resources, and human connections needed to maintain sobriety and live a fulfilling life.

  • Personalised relapse prevention planning
  • Regular follow-up calls and check-ins
  • Outpatient counselling and therapy sessions
  • Support group referrals (AA, NA, and others)
  • Family guidance and ongoing family counselling
  • Crisis support and re-admission if needed
