Who We Are

Healing Lives with
Compassion & Science

Nasha Mukti Kendra Dehradun is a premium rehabilitation centre dedicated to helping individuals break free from addiction and reclaim their lives. Our expert team combines medical science with holistic healing practices.

With a branch under Hugs Life Holistic in Greater Noida, we extend our care across North India — providing structured recovery programs that treat the body, mind, and spirit together.
