Treatment Programs

Structured, evidence-based programs tailored to different types of addiction and behavioural challenges.

Every person's journey into addiction is unique — and so is their path to recovery. Our treatment programs are designed to address specific substance dependencies and behavioural conditions with targeted, proven approaches. Each program combines medical treatment, psychological therapy, holistic healing, and structured aftercare.

Program 01

Alcohol De-addiction Program

Alcohol addiction is one of the most common and damaging forms of substance dependency. Our comprehensive alcohol de-addiction program guides patients from safe withdrawal through to full psychological and physical recovery.

The program addresses both the physical dependency on alcohol and the psychological patterns, emotional triggers, and lifestyle factors that sustain it. Recovery from alcohol addiction requires sustained effort — our program supports patients every step of the way.

Who This Program Helps

This program is suitable for individuals with mild, moderate, or severe alcohol use disorder, including those with prior failed attempts at recovery and those with co-occurring mental health conditions.

Treatment Phases

1
Assessment & Admission

Full medical and psychiatric evaluation, personalised treatment planning.

2
Medical Detox

Supervised alcohol withdrawal with medication to prevent complications.

3
Rehabilitation

Therapy, counselling, yoga, and psychiatric support for sustained recovery.

4
Aftercare

Relapse prevention, follow-up sessions, and family support.

Program 02

Drug De-addiction Program

Drug addiction encompasses a wide range of substances — from opioids and heroin to cocaine, cannabis, methamphetamine, prescription medications, and synthetic drugs. Our drug de-addiction program is tailored to the specific substance involved, as each presents different medical and psychological challenges.

Our team has extensive experience treating dependencies on all major substance categories, and uses medically proven detox and rehabilitation protocols for each.

Substances We Treat

  • Opioids & Heroin
  • Cannabis / Marijuana
  • Cocaine & Stimulants
  • Methamphetamine
  • Prescription Drug Misuse
  • Synthetic / Designer Drugs
  • Sedatives & Tranquillisers
  • Multiple Substance Dependencies

Treatment Phases

1
Substance-Specific Assessment

Identifying the substances used, duration, and intensity of dependency.

2
Medically Managed Detox

Safe, supervised withdrawal tailored to the specific substance and individual.

3
Intensive Rehab Program

Therapy, psychiatric care, and holistic healing over a structured residential stay.

4
Reintegration & Aftercare

Preparing for life post-treatment with relapse prevention and community support.

Program 03

Behavioural Disorders Program

Not all destructive patterns involve substances. Behavioural and emotional disorders — including anxiety, depression, anger management issues, compulsive behaviours, and mood disorders — can be just as disruptive to a person's life and relationships.

Our behavioural disorders program combines psychiatric treatment, psychotherapy, and holistic wellness to help individuals regain emotional balance, develop healthier coping strategies, and improve their quality of life.

Conditions We Address

  • Anxiety & Panic Disorders
  • Depression & Mood Disorders
  • Anger Management Issues
  • Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder (OCD)
  • Post-Traumatic Stress (PTSD)
  • Emotional Dysregulation
  • Compulsive Behaviours
  • Stress-Related Disorders

Treatment Approach

1
Psychiatric Evaluation

Comprehensive mental health assessment and diagnosis by our psychiatrist.

2
Personalised Therapy Plan

CBT, DBT, trauma therapy, and other evidence-based approaches.

3
Holistic Wellness

Yoga, meditation, and lifestyle adjustments to support mental health.

4
Ongoing Support

Regular follow-up, medication review, and family involvement.

🌟
Program 04

Teen Rehabilitation Program

Young people face unique pressures — peer influence, academic stress, family dynamics, and identity challenges — that can lead to substance use or behavioural problems. Early intervention is critical: the earlier treatment begins, the better the long-term outcome.

Our Teen Rehabilitation Program provides a compassionate, age-appropriate environment with therapists who specialise in working with young adults. We involve families deeply in the recovery process, as parental and family support is a key predictor of successful recovery in teenagers.

What Makes This Program Special

  • Separate, age-appropriate accommodation and activities
  • Therapists specialised in adolescent psychology
  • Academic support and educational continuity planning
  • Deep family involvement and family therapy sessions
  • Peer support groups for young adults
  • Life skills, self-esteem, and resilience building
  • Social media and technology addiction addressed

Treatment Phases

1
Family & Teen Assessment

Evaluation involving both the teenager and family to understand the full picture.

2
Safe & Supportive Detox

If required, medical detox in an age-appropriate setting with extra care.

3
Therapeutic Program

Age-adapted therapy, group sessions, and holistic activities for healing.

4
Family & Community Reintegration

Structured transition plan with school, family, and peer support.
