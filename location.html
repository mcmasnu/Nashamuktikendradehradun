Getting Here

How to Reach Our Dehradun Centre

Dehradun is well-connected by air, rail, and road from across North India.

✈️

By Air

Jolly Grant Airport (DED) is the nearest airport, about 30–35 km from Dehradun city. Taxis and cabs are readily available outside the terminal. Flights connect to Delhi, Mumbai, and other major cities.

🚂

By Train

Dehradun Railway Station is a major terminus with direct trains from Delhi (Shatabdi, Jan Shatabdi), Howrah, Mumbai, and other cities. The station is centrally located within the city.

🚌

By Road

Dehradun is approximately 300 km from Delhi via NH-58 (5–6 hours by car). ISBT Dehradun has regular Volvo and ordinary buses from Delhi, Haridwar, Rishikesh, and across Uttarakhand.
