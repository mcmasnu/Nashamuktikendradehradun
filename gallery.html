📸 Adding Your Photos

To replace the placeholders with your actual photos:

  1. Create an images/ folder inside your website directory.
  2. Add your photos to that folder (JPG or PNG recommended, max 2MB each).
  3. Open gallery.html in a text editor.
  4. Replace each <div class="gallery-placeholder"> block with:
    <img src="images/your-filename.jpg" alt="Your description">
  5. Upload the updated files to your GitHub repository.
