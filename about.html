About Hugs Life Holistic

A sanctuary of healing, hope, and transformation in the heart of Dehradun.

Our Mission

Restoring Lives, Rebuilding Families

At Hugs Life Holistic, our mission is simple: to provide every individual struggling with addiction a real, lasting path to recovery — with dignity, compassion, and expert care.

We believe addiction is not a moral failing but a complex health condition that requires professional treatment, emotional support, and a structured environment. Our team brings together medical expertise, psychological insight, and the healing power of holistic wellness practices.

We treat not just the addiction, but the whole person — addressing underlying trauma, mental health challenges, and the social dynamics that fuel dependency.

"Recovery is not a race. You don't have to feel guilty if it takes you longer than you thought it would."
— Our Treatment Philosophy

Our Core Values

What We Stand For

💙

Compassion First

Every patient is treated with kindness, respect, and non-judgmental care. We understand how difficult this journey is and walk it alongside you.

🔬

Evidence-Based Treatment

Our protocols follow medically proven practices — from safe detoxification to cognitive behavioural therapy and psychiatric medication management.

🌱

Holistic Wellness

True recovery goes beyond stopping substance use. We nurture mental, physical, and spiritual health through yoga, meditation, nutrition, and counselling.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family as Healer

Addiction affects the entire family. We actively involve loved ones in the recovery process through dedicated family therapy and counselling.

🔒

Complete Privacy

We maintain strict confidentiality for all patients and their families. Your journey with us is private and protected at all times.

🔄

Long-Term Recovery

Our work doesn't end at discharge. We provide structured aftercare, relapse prevention planning, and ongoing follow-up support.
